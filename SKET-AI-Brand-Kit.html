Logo Symbol: The 助 kanji carries three meanings — 且 (building/hospitality), 力 (power/strength), and the sweeping curve (modern, warm, forward-moving AI). Always maintain the orange-on-dark as the primary application. Never distort, recolour, or separate the wordmark from the symbol.
